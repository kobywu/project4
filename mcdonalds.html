What Mc Donald's is

Mc Donald's is a fast food franchise that has a limitless items to chose from. From different size and types of burgers to chicken nuggets to yogurt parfaits. They were built on the principle to have a small menu to focus on bettering the quality but have failed this plan through the years. Compared to In-N-Out they don't focus on opening their restaurants around the world but only in the west coast in the USA.
